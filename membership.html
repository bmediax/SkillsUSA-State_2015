ADVISORS AND MEMBERSHIP




    • Each Skills USA chapter is lead by a set of advisors. At Benson, our advisors are our technical teachers, lead by Cathy Skach, one of the current Digital Media instructors.

    All students in their 11th or 12th years who have declared a major are encouraged to join Skills USA. Those who are interested in membership may enquire through their major teacher, contact Cathy Skach, or otherwise contact our chapter through the contact form on this site.
